About

Nayaka Abhipraya or professionally called NYK. Verse / Nyk. Verse (Nick Verse) is a young boy that create music, animations, arts, fantasy story and more stuff. Nyk considers himself as "a Normal Young Kid that creates stuff" because of his name "NYK" which also means "Normal Young Kid" and "Verse" which means Universal and could also mean Forward. Nyk has a lot of nicknames from his friends and some nicknames that he made for himself. "Acro Potify" is one of the most used nicknames that Nyk uses for himself.

Nyk started music production in 2019 with his first mp3 song called "Just For U". He made "Just For U" for describing all the wonderful things about his ex-girlfriend.

After he made his first song "Just For U", He started to make a new one called "Angel". He writes lyrics of it but never sings cause he can't really sing much. "Angel" and "Just For U" are actually a sequel. Nyk started to produce and write more songs about bad feelings and happiness.

Nyk stopped making music for a while and editing some stuff such as "Zach Callison - War! Extended Lyric Video" that he uploaded into his YouTube named "NYK. Verse". His editing videos makes his channel grow faster and grow a lot of subscribers, but then he stopped uploading some editing videos but he upload more of his music to his YouTube with his 7th music called "Book Of Dream" and then continue with other music like "Never Left Me Behind", "Going Somewhere" and more.
