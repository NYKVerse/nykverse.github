About

Nayaka Abhipraya or professionally called NYK. Verse / Nyk. Verse (Nick Verse) is a young boy that create music, animations, arts, fantasy story and more stuff. Nyk consider himself as "a Normal Young Kid that create stuff" because of his name "NYK" that also means "Normal Young Kid" and "Verse" that means Universal and could also means Forward. Nyk has a lot of nicknames from his friends and some nicknames that he made for himself. "Acro Potify" is one of the most used nickname that Nyk use for himself.

Nyk started a music production since 2019 with his first mp3 song called "Just For U". He made "Just For U" for describing all wonderful things of his ex girlfriend.

After he made his first song "Just For U", He started to make a new one called "Angel". He write a lyrics of it but never sing cause he can't really sing much. "Angel" and "Just For U" is actually a siquel. Nyk started to produce and write more songs about bad feelings and happiness.

Nyk stopped making music for a while and editing some stuff such as "Zach Callison - War! Extended Lyric Video" that he uploaded into his YouTube named "NYK. Verse". His editing videos makes his channel grow faster and grow a lot of subscribers, but then he stopped upload some editing videos but he upload more of his music to his YouTube with his 7th music called "Book Of Dream" and then continue with other music like "Never Left Me Behind", "Going Somewhere" and more.
